About

Alethinos Music is a sync licensing composition house based in Whittier, California. We create emotionally charged, cinematic music for trailers, film scores, and game soundtracks.

Our sound sits at the intersection of orchestral grandeur and alternative intensity— lush ambient textures, driving rhythmic elements, and a willingness to embrace darkness when the story calls for it.
