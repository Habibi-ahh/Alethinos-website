About

Alethinos Music is a sync licensing composition house based in Whittier, California. We create emotionally charged, cinematic music for trailers, film scores, and game soundtracks.

Our sound sits at the intersection of orchestral grandeur and alternative intensity— lush ambient textures, driving rhythmic elements, and reflects the darkness of the world in which it was created.
